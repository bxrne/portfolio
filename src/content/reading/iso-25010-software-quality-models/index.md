The ISO/IEC 25010 standard defines quality models for software systems and their evaluation. It establishes eight product quality characteristics (functional suitability, performance efficiency, compatibility, usability, reliability, security, maintainability, portability) and five quality-in-use characteristics. Essential framework for specifying, measuring, and evaluating software quality across the development lifecycle.
